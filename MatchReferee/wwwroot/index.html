About Match Referee

Match Referee is dedicated to simplifying the process of connecting football coaches with qualified referees across the UK. Whether you're organizing a local match or seeking officiating opportunities, our platform makes it easy to arrange and manage match attendance.

For Referees

Discover match opportunities that suit your location and schedule. Connect with coaches, manage your bookings, and grow your officiating career.

For Coaches

Find trusted referees for your matches. Browse profiles, check locations and availability, and book officials with confidence to ensure your games run smoothly.

For Clubs

Organise matches efficiently by connecting with qualified referees. Manage bookings and ensure your club’s games are officiated professionally.

For Leagues

Streamline league operations by connecting with referees and coaches. Manage officiating with ease using our bulk upload feature.
