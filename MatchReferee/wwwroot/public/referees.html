How Match Referee can change your Weekends

Smart alerts that actually fit your life

Remember those endless group chats lighting up your phone at inconvenient times with games too far away, wrong level, or already taken? Now imagine opening the app to see only the matches that suit you — your local area, your preferred standard, your available weekends. One clean feed, no spam. Accept in seconds and the rest is handled automatically. You reclaim time that used to disappear into messaging.

Work with coaches you can actually trust

Last-minute cancellations are frustrating — they waste your time and disrupt your plans. On Match Referee you can see a coach's reliability feedback from previous referees before accepting a game. After every match you leave honest feedback, just as they do for you. Over time this helps you build relationships with dependable coaches who turn up, communicate clearly, and treat you well.

Your career story, all in one place

It’s hard to keep track of every coach you’ve worked with and how the day went. We keep your full match history for you — every date, every ground, every rating and note. It becomes your personal record: a clear overview of the games you’ve officiated and the relationships you’ve built. No more relying on memory when deciding which games to accept.

Let your quality shine through

In large group chats it’s easy for good referees to stay unnoticed. Here coaches can see your verified rating and badges earned through consistent feedback for punctuality, fairness and reliability. Your performance becomes visible to the people who matter — helping you stand out for the right reasons.

Paperwork? What paperwork?

Confirmations, reminders and chasing details used to take up your evenings. Now the platform handles the routine automatically: double confirmation, polite reminders at sensible times, everything tracked in one place. You arrive at the ground calm and focused — ready to referee rather than dealing with loose ends.

Local games find you

Many suitable local fixtures never reach you because they’re shared only through word-of-mouth or small groups. Our local feed shows available matches within a reasonable distance, sorted by level and proximity. You can discover opportunities closer to home that you might otherwise have missed.
