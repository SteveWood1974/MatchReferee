How Match Referee can change your Weekends

Book referees you can count on

Few things are more frustrating than a last-minute cancellation that leaves your team without an official. On Match Referee you can see reliability feedback from other coaches before accepting a referee. This helps you choose people with a track record of turning up and communicating clearly. Over time it becomes easier to find dependable officials for your fixtures.

Know they're actually coming

You've arranged everything — but then no one shows up on match day. The platform sends automatic reminders to the referee (48 hours and 2 hours before kick-off) with double confirmation steps. You get peace of mind knowing the official has acknowledged the game multiple times.

Choose referees who suit your level

Getting an official who struggles to manage the game can frustrate players and parents alike. Before booking you can see a referee's verified rating, feedback from previous coaches, and details like years officiating or match count. This lets you select someone experienced and well-regarded for your team's standard.

Find officials close to home

Travelling far for a referee adds unnecessary hassle and cost when local options might exist. Our local feed and map show available referees nearby, filtered by distance and rating. Booking someone from your area becomes straightforward and supports your local football community.

Everything in one conversation

Juggling texts, calls and emails to confirm details is time-consuming and messages get lost. In-app messaging keeps all communication in one secure thread — pitch location, parking, any special notes. No more searching through old chats when you need to check something quickly.

Remember who works well for you

After a few seasons it's hard to recall which referees were great (or not so great) with your team. Your personal history log shows every referee you've booked, with past ratings, notes and outcomes. Easily re-book your favourites and make informed decisions next time.
