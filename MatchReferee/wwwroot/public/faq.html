Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about MatchReferee – before and after our official launch on 1 July 2026.

General Questions

MatchReferee is a dedicated platform for grassroots football in the UK. Referees can manage availability and find local matches; coaches can post fixtures and secure referees; clubs can manage bookings, payments and team organisation. It's free for referees and coaches during the initial phase — clubs pay a low monthly subscription for advanced features.

The full live version launches on 1 July 2026. Until then, you can register, complete your profile, verify your email and explore the site. Some features (match posting, claiming, payments) are in preparation or disabled.

For Referees

After launch, referees set availability in their secure dashboard. Coaches post fixtures with requirements → matches appear in a local feed. You can claim open matches (first-come, first-served with confirmation). Double-confirmation prevents no-shows.

No — it's completely free for qualified referees. We may introduce optional premium features (e.g. priority visibility) in the future, but core use (availability, claiming matches, feedback) remains free.

For Coaches & Clubs

Coaches post fixtures via the secure area (date, time, venue, level, travel radius, fee). Referees see it in their feed and claim. You confirm or re-post if needed. Clubs with subscription can use templates, bulk posting and payment integration.

£20/month (billed via Stripe) unlocks advanced tools: unlimited fixture templates, team management, payment collection from parents/coaches, booking history, and priority support. Free trial period available post-launch.

Account, Payments & Technical

Sign up → verify email → complete profile (including uploading qualifications if referee). We manually review referee qualifications pre-launch for trust & safety.

We use double-confirmation and feedback/ratings after matches. Repeated no-shows lead to warnings or removal. Clubs can report issues via support.
