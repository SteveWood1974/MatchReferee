How Match Referee can support your league

Import fixtures quickly and accurately

Manually entering dozens — or hundreds — of games across divisions and venues takes hours and often leads to errors. Upload your full fixture list from a spreadsheet once. The system creates the schedule, shares it with clubs and referees, and keeps everything in sync.

Help ensure better venue coverage

Needing multiple referees for every pitch on busy central venue days, but struggling to fill them all, is a constant worry. Smart matching shows available officials by level, location and experience so you can more easily assign the right people to each game.

Reduce the impact of last-minute changes

A Friday night pull-out can derail an entire morning of games at a venue. Real-time availability lets you spot gaps early, and automated reminders help referees stay committed — making it easier to find replacements when needed.

Assign referees suited to the age group

Putting a less experienced official on a higher-age or more physical game can lead to issues on the pitch. You can filter and match referees based on their experience with U9s, U12s, U16s etc., so clubs get officials comfortable at the right level.

Cut down on endless texting and calls

Coordinating 20+ games via WhatsApp, emails and phone calls eats weekends and creates confusion. Once fixtures are uploaded, the platform handles assignments, confirmations and communication — freeing you to focus on league matters.

See real-time availability clearly

Guessing who’s free for Saturday morning U9s vs afternoon U16s leads to mistakes. Referees update their own calendars in the app, giving you and clubs instant visibility of who can take games — no more chasing replies.

Automate reminders and payments

Forgetting reminders or manually sorting fees creates extra work and frustration. Timed notifications keep everyone on track, and the system can track completed games to simplify referee payments or club invoicing.

One place to see everything

Tracking assignments, attendance, feedback and issues across divisions is scattered and stressful. Your league dashboard shows the full picture — upcoming games, who’s assigned, any gaps, and feedback trends — so you stay in control without spreadsheets.
