Privacy Policy

How MatchReferee collects, uses, protects and shares your personal information.

Last updated: 24 February 2026

MatchReferee (operated by 10th Dimension Limited) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, store and protect your personal data when you use our website and services (the "Platform"). It complies with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR), Data Protection Act 2018, and the Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations (PECR) as applicable in 2026.

The Platform is in pre-launch phase (official live date: 1 July 2026). Some features are not yet active.

2. What personal data we collect

We collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Account & Profile Data: Name, email address, phone number, role (referee/coach/club/league), password (hashed), profile photo, qualifications/certificates (for referees), availability calendar, bio/description, team/club details.
  • Payment & Subscription Data: Billing name/address, payment method details (handled securely by Stripe – we do not store full card numbers), subscription status, transaction history.
  • Usage & Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device info, pages visited, time/date of access, referral source (analytics via Firebase/Google).
  • Communication Data: Messages/emails sent via contact/support forms, feedback/ratings after matches (post-launch).

3. How we collect your data

  • Directly from you (registration, profile completion, contact form, payment setup).
  • Automatically (cookies, server logs, Firebase analytics – see our Cookie Policy link when implemented).
  • From third parties (Stripe for payments, email verification via Firebase).

5. Who we share your data with

  • Service providers: Firebase (Google LLC – database, auth, analytics; US-based with UK adequacy decision & safeguards), Stripe (payments; EU/UK entities), email delivery services.
  • Legal authorities if required by law.
  • We do not sell your data or share for marketing without consent.

6. International transfers

Some processors (e.g. Firebase/Google, Stripe) are based outside the UK/EU. We rely on UK adequacy regulations, standard contractual clauses, or other approved safeguards.

7. How long we keep your data

We retain data only as long as necessary:

  • Active accounts: while your account exists + 6 years after closure (legal/financial records).
  • Inactive accounts: deleted after 2 years of inactivity (unless you request earlier deletion).
  • Logs/analytics: up to 26 months (Google Analytics default).

8. Your rights under UK GDPR

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data (subject to legal exceptions)
  • Restrict processing
  • Object to processing (e.g. legitimate interests)
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent (where applicable)
  • Complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO): https://ico.org.uk

Contact us at privacy@matchreferee.co.uk to exercise rights. We respond within one month (extendable if complex).

9. Security

We use industry-standard measures (encryption in transit/rest, access controls, regular reviews). However, no system is 100% secure.
